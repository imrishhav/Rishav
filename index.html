    About Me


    Hey, I’m Rishu Rishav — a passionate self-taught web developer, ethical hacker-in-training, and dreamer who believes in creating value through creativity, code, and compassion. I come from humble beginnings, driven by a deep desire to build something meaningful — not just for myself, but for those who feel unseen in this fast world. I didn’t have everything figured out, but I chose to start, to learn, and to grow every single day. I’ve spent sleepless nights learning how to design, build, and secure websites that not only look good but speak to people. My journey isn’t just about websites or code — it’s about freedom, self-expression, and impact. I’m building this platform as a reflection of my values: simplicity, depth, and authenticity. I believe in silent hard work, letting actions speak, and always showing up — for myself and for others. Through every project I create, I aim to combine emotion with technology — so that even a single click or scroll can tell a story. Thanks for visiting my world. I’m just getting started.
